Topics

Ghosts

Stories relating to apparitions, poltergeists, and, Aaron's personal favorite, hauntings. Stories that delve deep into spectres and their context, especially tales of their malevolence.

Killers

Tales about murderers and killers - most of them intentional. From H.H. Holmes to the Villisca Axe Murderer, these stories explore the dark minds that performed the most nefarious deeds.

Cryptids

Indulge your curiosity and explore the local folklore behind some of the better-know cryptic creatures. These tales relate some accounts and sightings of secretive, sometimes terrifying creatures like the Jersey Devil and the Dover Demon.

Occult

These stories explore occultism, witchcraft, and other pagan occurrences. We delve deep into the folklore and local tales behind figures like Marie Laveau in New Orleans and the Salem Witch trials.

Creatures

These stories explore creatures from mytholigy, which often have the deepest roots in local folklore. You'll find explorations behind some of culture's better-known demons, like vampires and werewolves. We also explore at-legnth the relationship betwen societies and elves, fairies, changelings, and wendigos.
