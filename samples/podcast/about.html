About the Podcast

Lore is an award-winning podcast that focuses on non-fiction, true life scary stories. Lore explores historical events that show the dark side of human nature, using folklore as its primary lens.

Each episode fouces on a new, dark historical tale using a style that the creator himself calls a "campfire experience." New episodes are released every two weeks on Mondays.

The series was created in 2015 by Aaron Mahnke as a marketing experiment, and quickly amassed a captive audience and critical acclaim. It received the iTunes "Best of 2015" Award, the "Best History Podcast" award in 2016, and was included in the end-of-year top lists by The Atlantic. You can also find the TV series adaptation on Prime Video over at Amazon.

About Aaron Mahnke

Undoubtedly the persona that gives Lore its edge, Aaron Mahnke is the creator, producer, and host of the hit podcast Lore , Executive Producer of the Lore television show on Amazon (from the producers of The Walking Dead), and author of The World of Lore book series (Penguin Random House / Del Rey).

Born and raised in Illinois, Aaron now lives with his wife and children on the historic North Shore of Boston, where he writes and records full-time.
